About Me

I have a broad research interest in theoretical physics with an emphasis on the properties of the extremely hot and dense forms of matter, e.g. quark gluon plasma. A list of my publications can be found at inSPIRES

I am currently a scientist at Quark Matter Research Center, Institute of Modern Physics (Lanzhou), Chinese Academy of Sciences. Previously, I was a postdoctoral research associate at the Center for Theoretical Physics, MIT. Here is my CV.

In the long run, I will aim at employing the lesson we learned about the quark-gluon matter to understand the mystery of other strongly interacting systems.
